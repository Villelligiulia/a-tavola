Spaghetti alle Vongole, or spaghetti with clams, is a coastal favorite that hails from Naples, celebrated for its delicate yet vibrant flavors. The dish combines fresh clams, garlic, olive oil, white wine, and a touch of red chili pepper, creating a light yet savory sauce that enhances the natural sweetness of the clams. The briny flavor of the sea infuses each bite, mingling beautifully with the al dente pasta, fresh parsley, and a hint of lemon zest.

This dish is a celebration of Italy’s coastline, capturing the essence of the Mediterranean with its fresh ingredients and minimal preparation. Simple, aromatic, and refreshing, Spaghetti alle Vongole brings a taste of the sea to the table, embodying the Italian commitment to showcasing high-quality, local ingredients.

Ingredienti

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 kg fresh clams
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparazione

  1. Soak clams in salted water for 20 minutes, then rinse.
  2. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  3. Sauté garlic in olive oil, add clams, and cook until they open.
  4. Add drained spaghetti and fresh parsley to the pan, tossing well.
  5. Season with pepper, drizzle with olive oil, and serve immediately.
