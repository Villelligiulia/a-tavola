Parmigiana di Melanzane, or Eggplant Parmesan, is a comforting Italian classic from southern Italy, especially Campania and Sicily. This dish layers thin slices of fried eggplant with rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and grated Parmigiano, then bakes it all to golden perfection. The tender eggplant and tangy tomato sauce balance beautifully with the creamy cheese, creating a hearty yet refined dish.

The dish is a true expression of Italian cooking, transforming humble ingredients into a flavorful, satisfying meal. Parmigiana di Melanzane embodies the warmth and richness of southern Italian cuisine, perfect for sharing and savoring with family and friends around the table.

Ingredienti

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Salt to taste
  • Olive oil for frying or baking
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Preparazione

  1. Slice eggplants, salt them, and let them drain for 30 minutes. Pat dry.
  2. Fry or bake eggplant slices until golden, then set aside.
  3. Layer eggplant, tomato sauce, mozzarella, and grated Parmigiano in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
